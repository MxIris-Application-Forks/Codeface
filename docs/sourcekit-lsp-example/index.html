Visualizing SourceKit-LSP

Here are 19 scopes in Apple's LSP server visualized with the Codeface.io app. We see their hierarchical composition and essential dependencies.

Artifacts over which the mouse hovers have blue borders and blue ingoing and outgoing dependencies. But the mouse is invisible in the screenshots.
